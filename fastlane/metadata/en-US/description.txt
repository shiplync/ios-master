Traansmission is a real-time, location-based system that gives shippers and truckers the power of direct communication — without a broker

・ Find a load using the app and get freight within hours. Location-based service means no “deadheads” and no waiting hours on brokers to call back.

・ Easily set your search location on the map, even if you don't know the exact address. Get connected to your shipper and send the progress of your movement at any time.

・ Sit back, relax, and take any load you want. We'll email you a receipt when you arrive at your destination.
